Privacy Butler

The legal conditions of services
you rely on every day placed into
the capable hands of an A.I. agent

  • Tell me more

The Privacy Butler aims to help you to understand any privacy notice through the use of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. This is a concept-stage project with a prototype that won 2nd place at the Swiss Legal Tech 2018 hackathon.

  • Check out the full concept pitch from the hackathon.
  • Find a list of similar & recommended tools in our documentation.
  • Download the open source code and follow our technical instructions to test the (cloud ML based) prototype.
  • Read our data science notebook to see details of an (offline ML based) alternative approach.
  • See the challenge statement that motivated this project.
  • If you have questions or interest in seeing this further developed, star the project and open an Issue on GitHub. Or ping us in the #privacy-butler channel on the hacking.law Slack.
  • We also have a newsletter you can join for future updates.
